TIMETRACK: GPS Timekeeping System
Developed for Onsite, Remote, and Hybrid Working Employees of Barangay Pulong Buhangin, Santa Maria, Bulacan
Using PHP and MySQLi

Features:
- Attendance Taking through Geolocation and Geofencing
- One Time Passcode Verification through Email
- Employee Panel for Managing Attendance, Personal Time Records, and Personal Leaves
- Employer Panel for Managing Employees, Time Records, and Leaves
